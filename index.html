Who We Are

Rooted in Craft

It started with a Moka pot and a stubborn belief that great coffee doesn't need to be complicated — just honest.

We work directly with small-holder farmers across Coorg, Chikmagalur, and Araku Valley, buying at fair prices and roasting in small batches every week.

No shortcuts. No burnt beans hidden under flavoured syrups. Just terroir you can taste.
